About

Esmé Puzio is a recent graduate from the University of California, Santa Barbara, with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and a minor in Art. She is interested in post-internet art, web development, UI/UX design and the ways in which art and technology can complement one another. She has worked with the Expressive Computation Lab to develop JavaScript support for digital fabrication technology and has created AI-inspired artwork under Dr. Helen Taschian.

In her free time, she enjoys rock climbing, cooking and watching obscure movies with friends.
