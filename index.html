About

Esmé Puzio is a fourth-year Computer Science student at the University of California, Santa Barbara. She's interested in post-internet art, UI/UX design, and the ways in which art and technology can complement one another. As an artist, Esmé enjoys making colorful and communicative designs, and she has been illustrating articles for the Daily Nexus newspaper since 2021.
