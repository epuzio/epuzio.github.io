Esmé Puzio is currently pursuing her B.S. in Computer Science at the University of California, Santa Barbara, with a minor in Art. She's interested in post-internet art, UI/UX design, and the ways in which art and technology can complement and further develop one another. As an artist, she enjoys making colorful and communicative designs, and has been illustrating articles for the Daily Nexus newspaper since 2021.
